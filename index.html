interMission

Mission

interMission is a community that cultivates the sacred time between life's seasons through engaging in spiritual practices to set our souls aflame with love for Jesus and a vision to serve the world in His holy name.

Weekly Rhythms of Formation

Connect

  • Share life journeys and spiritual experiences
  • Reflect on previous week's practices and insights

Pray

  • Seek personal renewal / awakening
  • Intercede for a move of God in our church, community, and the world
  • Listen for the Holy Spirit's voice and leading

Learn

  • Engage with Scripture
  • Explore historical and contemporary spiritual practices and perspectives
  • Understand the theological foundations of formation

Practice

  • Cultivate habits and perspectives that are life-giving and character forming
  • Apply spiritual practices intentionally, both within and beyond our comfort zones

Go

  • Expect God to use you in the every day
  • Live out your purpose as God's handiwork, glorifying Him through the good works He prepared in advance for you to do

Time Commitment

We believe spiritual formation requires intentional investment. Each week includes our 2-hour group gathering plus personal spiritual practices that could take anywhere from 30 minutes to 5 hours, depending on the week's focus and your availability. Most weeks will require much less than the maximum, but some practices like preparing your life journey or extended times of solitude may ask more of you. We're committed to being realistic about what this journey entails so you can make an informed decision about joining us.

Interested?

If you're interested in learning more, you're encouraged to read through the tenatitve schedule and the group covenant. If you're still interested, please fill out the brief application / interest form. It should take less than 15 minutes.
